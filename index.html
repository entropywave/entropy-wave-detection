Entropy Wave Detection Initiative

Founder & Research Lead: Steve Pence

This project explores the use of passive quantum coherence monitoring to detect global entropy-relevant field fluctuations.

Our primary paper is titled: Global Phase Coherence Drift as a Probe of Cosmological Entropy Fluctuations.

We welcome collaboration from quantum labs and research institutions. For more details, please review the README.md in our GitHub repository.
